Ingredients:
 - 1 cup of rice
 - 1 spoon of salt
 - 1 spoon of oil
 - 1 spoon of carry
 - 2 spoons of sugar

Directions:
 1. preheat the oil with the sugar till it gets brow
 2. Don't forget to stir
 3. when the sugar is brown add the rice (you may need to refer to preparing the rice)
 4. add 1 cup of boiling water when the rice is ready
 5. cover and wait for about 20 mins
